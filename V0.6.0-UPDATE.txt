CardManagerPro v0.6.0 update

Included in this build:
- Scan Inbox full-screen zoom with mouse-wheel zoom and drag-to-pan.
- Scan thumbnails show review guidance for player, year, set and card number.
- Choose accepted scan or stock image as the primary inventory image.
- The accepted scan remains attached even when stock is the preferred display image.
- Checklist CSV import can include Stock Image URL.
- Checklist import can upload multiple JPG/PNG/WEBP stock image files named by card number.
- Complete-set collection builder with search, missing-only filter, keyboard quantity entry and bulk inventory creation.
- Existing raw inventory quantities are increased instead of creating unnecessary duplicates.
- Inventory thumbnails honor the selected scan/stock image preference.
