CardManager Pro v0.7.0 - Scanner and Import Workspace

New:
- Upload multiple scans directly into the scanner inbox.
- Pair front and back images before import.
- Zoom with mouse wheel, pan by dragging, rotate, and reset.
- Search existing inventory and reference checklists before saving.
- Select a match to automatically populate player, team, year, set, card number, variation and stock image.
- Add a card to Inventory, Checklist, or both.
- Increase quantity on an existing matched inventory record instead of making a duplicate.
- Keep scan and stock-image options.
- Version badge updated to v0.7.0.

Database:
- No new migration is required for this release.
